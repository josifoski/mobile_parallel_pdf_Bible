{42:1} tab ayyoob yahova ko uttar diya
{42:2} maain janata hoo ki too sab kuchh kar sakata haai, aur teree yuktaiyon men se koi ruk naheen sakatee.
{42:3} too kaun haai jo gyaan raahit hokar yuaktai par parada dalata haai? parantu maain ne to jo naheen samajhta tha vahee kaha, arthata jo baten mere liye aadhaik kattnai aur meree samajh se bahar theen jinako maain janata bhee naheen tha.
{42:4} maain nivedan karata hoon sun, maain kuchh kahoonga, maain tujh se prashn karata hoo, too mujhe bata de.
{42:5} maain kanon se tera samachar suna tha, parantu ab meree ankhen tujhe dekhtee haain
{42:6} isaliye mujhe apane upar gharana atee haai, aur maain dhooli aur rakh men pashchattap karata hoo.
{42:7} aur aeesa hua ki jab yahova ye baten ayyoob se kah chuka, tab us ne temanee aeleepaj se kaha, mera krodha tere aur tere donon mitraen par bhdka haai, kyonki jaaisee tteek bat mere das ayyoob ne mere vishay kahee haai, vaaisee tum logon ne naheen kahee.
{42:8} isaliye ab tum sat baail aur sat meddhe chhanttkar mere das ayyoob ke pas jakar apane nimitt homabaali chaddhao, tab mera das ayyoob tumhare liye praarthna karega, kyonki usee kee maain grahan karoonga aur nahee, to maain tum se tumharee mooddhta ke yogy bartav karoonga, kyonki tum logon ne mere vishay mere das ayyoob kee see tteek bat naheen kahee.
{42:9} yah sun temanee aeleepaj, shoohee bildad aur namatee sopar ne jakar yahova kee aagyaa ke anusar kiya, aur yahova ne ayyoob kee praarthna grahan kee.
{42:10} jab ayyoob ne apane mitraen ke liye praarthna kee, tab yahorava ne usaka sara dukh door kiya, aur jitana ayyoob ka paahile tha, usaka dugana yahova ne use de diya.
{42:11} tab usake sab bhai, aur sab bahine, aur jitane pahile usako janate paahichanate the, un sabhon ne akar usake yahan usake sang bhojan kiya aur jitanee vipaattai yahova ne us par dalee thee, us sab ke vishay unhon ne vilap kiya, aur use shaaantai dee aur use aek aek sikka or sone kee aek aek balee dee.
{42:12} aur yahova ne ayyoob ke pichhle dinon men usako agale dinon se aadhaik ashaeesh dee aur usake chaudah hajar bhend bakariya, chhhajar unt, hajar jodee baail, aur hajar gadaahiyan ho gai.
{42:13} aur usake sat bette or teen bettyaian bhee utpann hui.
{42:14} in men se us ne jettee bettee ka nam to yameema, doosaree ka kaseea aur teesaree ka kerenhappook rakha.
{42:15} aur us sare desh men aensee striayan kaheen n thee, jo ayyoob kee beattyaien ke saman sundar ho, aur unake pita ne unako unake bhaiyon ke sang hee sampati dee.
{42:16} isake bad ayyoob aek sau chalees varsha jeevit raha, aur char peeddhee tak apana vansh dekhne paya.
{42:17} nidan ayyoob vraddhavastha men deegharayu hokar mar gaya.
