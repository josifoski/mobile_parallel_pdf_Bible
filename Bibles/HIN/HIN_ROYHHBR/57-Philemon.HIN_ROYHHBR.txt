{1:1} paulus kee or se jo maseeh yeeshu ka kaaidee haai, aur bhai timuathaiyus kee or se hamare piry sahakarmee filemona.
{1:2} aur baahin afaafiya, aur hamare sathee yoddha arakhaippus aur filemon ke ghar kee kaleeasiya ke nama..
{1:3} hamare pita parameshvar aur prabhu yeeshu maseeh kee or se anugrah aur shaaantai tumhen milatee rahe..
{1:4} maain tere us praem aur vishvas kee charcha sunakar, jo sab pavitra logon ke sath aur prabhu yeeshu par haai.
{1:5} sada parameshvar ka dhanyavad karata hoon aur apanee praarthnaon men bhee tujhe smaran karata hoon.
{1:6} ki tera vishvas men sahabhagee hona tumharee saree bhlai kee pahichan men maseeh ke liye prabhavashaalee ho.
{1:7} kyonaki he bhai, mujhe tere praem se bahut anand aur shaantai milee, isaliye, ki tere dara pavitra logon ke man hare bhre ho gaae haain..
{1:8} isaliye yadhypi mujhe maseeh men bada hiyav to haai, ki jo bat tteek haai, us kee agyaa tujhe doon.
{1:9} taubhee mujh booddhe paulus ko jo ab maseeh yeeshu ke liye kaaidee hoo, yah aur bhee bhla jan pada ki praem se binatee karoon.
{1:10} maain apane bachche uneasimus ke liye jo mujh se meree kaaid men janma haai tujh se binatee karata hoon.
{1:11} vah to paahile tere kuchh kam ka n tha, par ab tere aur mere donon ke bade kam ka haai.
{1:12} usee ko arthata jo mere day ka ttukada haai, maain ne use tere pas lautta diya haai.
{1:13} use maain apane hee pas rakhna chahata tha ki teree or se is kaaid men jo susamachar ke karan haai, meree seva kare.
{1:14} par maain ne teree ichchha bina kuchh bhee karana n chaha ki teree yah krapa dabav se naheen par anand se ho.
{1:15} kyonaki kya jane vah tujh se kuchh din tak ke liye isee karan alag hua ki sadaaiv tere nikatt rahe.
{1:16} parantu ab se das kee nai nahee, baran das se bhee uttm, arthata bhai ke saman hare jo shareer men bhee aur vishosh kar prabhu men bhee mera piry ho.
{1:17} so yaadi too mujhe sahabhagee samajhta haai, to use is prakar grahan kar jaaise mujhe.
{1:18} aur yaadi us ne teree kuchh haani kee haai, ya us par tera kuchh ata haai, to mere nam par likh le.
{1:19} maain paulus apane hath se likhta hoo, ki maain ap bhr doonga aur ham ke kahane kee kuchh avashyakata nahee, ki mera karja jo tujh par haai vah too hee haai.
{1:20} he bhai yah anand mujhe prabhu men teree or se mile: maseeh men mere jee ko hara bhra kar de.
{1:21} maain tere agyaakaree hone ka bhrosa rakhkar, tujhe likhta hoon aur yah janata hoo, ki jo kuchh maain kahata hoo, too us se kaheen baddhkar karega.
{1:22} aur yah bhee, ki mere liye utarane kee jagah taaiyar rakh mujhe asha haai, ki tumharee praarthnaon ke dara maain tumhen de diya jaunga..
{1:23} ipaas jo maseeh yeeshu men mere sath kaaidee haai.
{1:24} aur marakus aur aaristarkhus aur demas aur looka jo mere sahakarmee haai in ka tujhe namaskara..
{1:25} hamare prabhu yeeshu maseeh ka anugrah tumharee atma par hota rahe. ameena..
