{1:1} paulus aur silavanus aur teemuthaiyus kee or se thaissaluanikiyon kee kaleesiya ke nam jo parameshvar pita aur prabhu yeeshu maseeh men haai.. anugrah aur shaaantai tumhen milatee rahe..
{1:2} ham apanee praarthnaon men tumhen smaran karate aur sada tum sab ke vishay men parameshvar ka dhanyavad karate haain.
{1:3} aur apane parameshvar aur pita ke samhane tumhare vishvas ke kam, aur praem ka parishram, aur hamare prabhu yeeshu maseeh men asha kee dhaeerata ko lagatar smaran karate haain.
{1:4} aur he bhaiyo, parameshvar ke piry logon ham janaten haai, ki tum chune huae ho.
{1:5} kyonaki hamara susamachar tumhare pas n keval vachan matra hee men baran samarth aur paavitra atma, aur bade nishchay ke sath pahuncha haai jaaisa tum janate ho, ki ham tumhare liye tum men kaaise ban gaae the.
{1:6} aur tum bade klesh men paavitra atma ke anand ke sath vachan ko manakar hamaree aur prabhu kee see chal chalane lage.
{1:7} yahan tak ki makiduaniya aur akhya ke sab vishvasiyon ke liye tum adarsha bane.
{1:8} kyonaki tumhare yahan se n keval makiduaniya aur akhya men prabhu ka vachan sunaya gaya, par tumhare vishvas kee jo parameshvar par haai, har jagah aeesee charcha faail gai haai, ki hamen kahane kee avashyakata hee naheen.
{1:9} kyonaki ve ap hee hamare vishay men batate haain ki tumhare pas hamara ana kaaisa hua aur tum kyonaki mooraton se parameshvar kee or firen taaki jeevate aur sachche parameshvar kee seva karo.
{1:10} aur usake putra ke svarga par se ane kee batt johate raho jise us ne mare huon men se jilaya, arthata yeeshu kee, jo hamen anevale prakop se bachata haai..
{2:1} he bhaiyo, tum ap hee janate ho ki hamara tumhare pas ana vyarth n hua.
{2:2} baran tum ap hee janate ho, ki pahile paahil filippee men dukh uttane aur upadrav sahane par bhee hamare parameshvar ne hamen aeesa hiyav diya, ki ham parameshvar ka susamachar bharee virodhaen ke hote huae bhee tumhen sunaaen.
{2:3} kyonaki hamara upadesh n bhram se haai aur n ashuuddhta se, aur n chhl ke sath haai.
{2:4} par jaaisa parameshvar ne hamen yogy ttharakar susamachar saunpa, ham vaaisa hee varnn karate haain aur is men manushyon ko nahee, parantu parameshvar ko, jo hamare manon ko janchata haai, prasann karate haain.
{2:5} kyonaki tum janate ho, ki ham n to kabhee lallopato kee baten kiya karate the, aur n lobh ke liye bahana karate the, parameshvar gavah haai.
{2:6} aur yadhypi ham maseeh ke praerit hone ke karan tum par bojh dal sakate the, taubhee ham manushyon se adar naheen chahate the, aur n tum se, n aur kisee se.
{2:7} parantu jis tarah mata apane balakon ka palana-poshan karatee haai, vaaise hee ham ne bhee tumhare beech men rahakar komalata dikhai haai.
{2:8} aur vaaise hee ham tumharee lalasa karate hua, n keval parameshvar ko susamachar, par apana apana praan bhee tumhen dene ko taaiyar the, isaaliye ki tum hamare pyare ho gaae the.
{2:9} kyonaki, he bhaiyo, tum hamare parishram aur kashtt ko smaran rakhte ho, ki ham ne isaaliye rat din kam andha karate huae tum men parameshvar ka susamachar prachar kiya, ki tum men se kisee par bhar n hon.
{2:10} tum ap hee gavah ho: aur parameshvar bhee, ki tumhare beech men jo vishvas rakhte ho ham kaaisee pavitrata aur dhaaarmikata aur nirdoshata se rahe.
{2:11} jaaise tum janate ho, ki jaaisa pita apane balakon ke sath bartav karata haai, vaaise hee ham tum men se har aek ko bhee upadesh karate, aur shaaantai dete, aur samajhate the.
{2:12} ki tumhara chal chalan parameshvar ke yogy ho, jo tumhen apane rajy aur mahima men bulata haai..
{2:13} isaliye ham bhee parameshvar ka dhanyavad nirantar karate haain ki jab hamare dara parameshvar ke susamachar ka vachan tumhare pas pahuncha, to tum ne us manushyon ka nahee, parantu parameshvar ka vachan samajhkar aur sachamuch yah aeesa hee haai grahan kiya: aur vah tum men jo vishvas rakhte ho, prabhavashaalee haai.
{2:14} isaliye ki tum, he bhaiyo, parameshvar kee un kaleesiyaon kee see chal chalane lage, jo yahoodiya men maseeh yeeshu men haai, kyonki tum ne bhee apane logon se vaaisa hee dukh paya, jaaisa unhon ne yahoodiyon se paya tha.
{2:15} jinhon ne prabhu yeeshu ko aur bhvishyadvktaon ko bhee mar dala aur ham ko sataya, aur parameshvar un se prasann naheen aur ve sab manushyon ko virodha karate haain.
{2:16} aur ve anyajatiyon se un ke uddhar ke liye baten karane se hamen rokate haai, ki sada apane papon ka napua bhrate rahen par un par bhyanak prakop a pahuncha haai..
{2:17} he bhaiyo, jab ham thodee der ke liye man men naheen baran pragatt men tum se alag ho gaae the, to ham ne badee lalasa ke sath tumhara munh dekhne ke liye aur bhee aadhaik yatn kiya.
{2:18} isaliye ham ne arthata mujh paulus ne aek bar nahee, baran do bar tumhare pas ana chaha, parantu shautan hamen roke raha.
{2:19} bhla hamaree asha, ya anand ya badai ka mukutt kya haai? kya hamare prabhu yeeshu ke sammukh usake ane ke samay tum hee n hoge?
{2:20} hamaree badai aur anand tum hee ho..
{3:1} isaliye jab ham se aur bhee n raha gaya, to ham ne yah ttharaya ki aethens men akele rah jaaen.
{3:2} aur ham ne teemuthaiyus ko jo maseeh ke susamachar men hamara bhai, aur parameshvar ka sevak haai, isaaliye bheja, ki vah tumhen sthiar kare aur tumhare vishvas ke vishay men tumhen samajhaae.
{3:3} ki koi in kleshaen ke karan dgamaga n jaae kyonaki tum ap janate ho, ki ham in hee ke liye ttharaae gaae haain.
{3:4} kyonaki pahile bhee, jab ham tumhare yahan the, to tum ne kaha karate the, ki hamen klesh uttane padenge, aur aeesa hee hua haai, aur tum janate bhee ho.
{3:5} is karan jab mujh se aur n raha gaya, to tumhare vishvas ka hal janane ke liye bheja, ki kaheen aeesa n ho, ki pareeksha karanevale ne tumharee pareeksha kee ho, aur hamara paarishram vyarth ho gaya ho.
{3:6} par abhee teemuathaiyus ne jo tumhare pas se hamare yahan akar tumhare vishvas aur praem ka susamachar sunaya aur is bat ko bhee sunaya, ki tum sada praem ke sath hamen smaran karate ho, aur hamare dekhne kee lalasa rakhte ho, jaaisa ham bhee tumhen dekhne kee.
{3:7} isaliye he bhaiyo, ham ne apanee saree saketee aur klesh men tumhare vishvas se tumhare vishay men shaantai pai.
{3:8} kyonaki ab yadi tum prabhu men sthiar raho to ham jeevit haain.
{3:9} aur jaaisa anand hamen tumhare karan apane parameshvar ke samhane haai, usake badale tumhare vishay men ham kis reeti se parameshvar ka dhanyavad karen?
{3:10} ham raj din bahut hee praarthna karate rahate haai, ki tumhara munh dekhe, aur tumhare vishvas kee ghattee pooree karen..
{3:11} ab hamara parameshvar aur pita ap hee aur hamara prabhu yeeshu, tumhare yahan ane ke liye hamaree aguvai kare.
{3:12} aur prabhu aeesa kare, ki jesa ham tum se praem rakhte haain vaaisa hee tumhara praem bhee apas me, aur sab manushyon ke sath baddhe, aur unnaati karata jaae.
{3:13} taaki vah tumhare manon ko aeesa sthiar kare, ki jab hamara prabhu yeeshu apane sab pavitra logon ke sath aa, to ve hamare parameshvar aur pita ke samhane pavitrata men nirdosh ttharen..
{4:1} nidan, he bhaiyo, ham tum se binatee karate haai, aur tumhen prabhu yeeshu men samajhate haai, ki jaaise tum ne ham se yogy chal chalana, aur parameshvar ko prasann karana seekha haai, aur jaaisa tum chalate bhee ho, vaaise hee aur bhee baddhte jao.
{4:2} kyonaki tum janate ho, ki ham ne prabhu yeeshu kee or se tumhen kaun kaun see agyaa pahunchai.
{4:3} kyonaki parameshvar kee ichchha yah haai, ki tum pavitra bano: arthata vyaabhaichar se bache raho.
{4:4} aur tum men se har aek paavitrata aur adar ke sath apane patra ko praapt karana jane.
{4:5} aur yah kam aabhailasha se nahee, aur n un jaatiyon kee nai, jo parameshvar ko naheen janateen.
{4:6} ki is bat men koi apane bhai ko n ttge, aur n us par danv chalaa, kyonaki prabhu is sab baton ka palatta lenevala haai jaaisa ki ham ne pahile tum se kaha, aur chitaya bhee tha.
{4:7} kyonaki parameshvar ne hamen ashuuddh hone ke liye nahee, parantu paavitra hone ke liye bulaya haai.
{4:8} is karan jo tuchchh janata haai, vah manushy ko nahee, parantu parameshvar ko tuchchh janata haai, jo apana paavitra atma tumhen deta haai..
{4:9} kintu bhaichare kee praeeti ke vishay men yah avashy nahee, ki maain tumhare pas kuchh likoon kyonaki apas men praem rakhna tum ne ap hee parameshvar se seekha haai.
{4:10} aur sare makiduaniya ke sab bhaiyon ke sath aeesa karate bhee ho, par he bhaiyo, ham tumhen samajhate haai, ki aur bhee baddhte jao.
{4:11} aur jaaisee ham ne tumhen agyaa dee, vaaise hee chupachap rahane aur apana apana kam kaj karane, aur apane apane hathon se kamane ka prayatn karo.
{4:12} ki baharavalon ke sath sabhyata se bartav karo, aur tumhen kisee vastu kee ghattee n ho..
{4:13} he bhaiyo, ham naheen chahate, ki tum unake vishay men jo sote haai, agyaan raho aeesa n ho, ki tum auron kee nai shaek karo jinhen asha naheen.
{4:14} kyonaki yadi ham prateeati karate haai, ki yeeshu mara, aur jee bhee utta, to vaaise hee parameshvar unhen bhee jo yeeshu men so gaae haai, usee ke sath le aaega.
{4:15} kyonaki ham prabhu ke vachan ke anusar tum se yah kahate haai, ki ham jo jeevit haai, aur prabhu ke ane tak bache rahenge to soae huon se kabhee age n baddhenge.
{4:16} kyonaki prabhu ap hee svarga se utarega us samay lalakar, aur pradhaan doot ka shabd sunai dega, aur parameshvar kee turahee foonkee jaaegee, aur jo maseeh men mare haai, ve pahile jee uttenge.
{4:17} tab ham jo jeeavit aur bache rahenge, un ke sath badalon par utta liae jaaenge, ki hava men prabhu se mile, aur is reeti se ham sada prabhu ke sath rahenge.
{4:18} so in baton se aek doosare ko shaantai diya karo..
{5:1} par he bhaiyo, isaka prayojan nahee, ki samayon aur kalon ke vishay men tumhare pas kuchh likha jaae.
{5:2} kyonaki tum ap tteek janate ho ki jaaisa rat ko chor ata haai, vaaisa hee prabhu ka din anevala haai.
{5:3} jab log kahate honge, ki kushal haai, aur kuchh bhy nahee, to un par aekaaek vinash a padega, jis prakar garbhvatee par peeda aur ve kisee reeati se bachenge.
{5:4} par he bhaiyo, tum to andhakar men naheen ho, ki vah din tum par chor kee nai a pade.
{5:5} kyonaki tum sab jyoti kee santan, aur din kee santan ho, ham n rat ke haai, n andhakar ke haain.
{5:6} isaliye ham auron kee nai sote n rahe, par jagate aur savadhaan rahen.
{5:7} kyonaki jo sote haai, ve rat hee ko soten haai, aur jo matavale hote haai, ve rat hee ko matavale hote haain.
{5:8} par ham to din ke haai, vishvas aur praem kee jhlaim pahinakar aur uddhar kee ttop paahinakar savadhaan rahen.
{5:9} kyonaki parameshvar ne hamen krodha ke liye nahee, parantu isaliye ttharaya ki ham apane prabhu yeeshu maseeh ke dara uddhar praapt karen.
{5:10} vah hamare liye is karan mara, ki ham chahe jagate ho, chahe sote hon: sab milakar usee ke sath jeeaen.
{5:11} is karan aek doosare ko shaantai do, aur aek doosare kee unnaati ke karan bano, nidan, tum aeesa karate bhee ho..
{5:12} aur he bhaiyo, ham tum se binatee karate haai, ki jo tum men parishram karate haai, aur prabhu men tumhare aguve haai, aur tumhen shaiksha dete haai, unhen mano.
{5:13} aur un ke kam ke karan praem ke sath un ko bahut hee adar ke yogy samajho: apas men mela-amilap se raho.
{5:14} aur he bhaiyo, ham tumhen samajhate haai, ki jo tteek chal naheen chalate, un ko samajhao, kayaron ko ddhaddhs do, nirbalon ko sanbhalo, sab kee or sahanashaeelata dikhao.
{5:15} savadhaana! koi kisee se burai ke badale burai n kare par sada bhlai karane par tatpar raho apas men aur sab se bhee bhlai hee kee cheshtta karo.
