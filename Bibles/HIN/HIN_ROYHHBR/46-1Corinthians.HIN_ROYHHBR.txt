{16:13} jagate raho, vishvas men sthiar raho, purooshaarth karo, balavant hoo.
{16:14} jo kuchh karate ho praem se karo..
{16:15} he bhaiyo, tum staifanas ke gharane ko janate ho, ki ve akhya ke pahile fal haai, aur pavitra logon kee seva ke liye taaiyar rahate haain.
{16:16} so maain tum se binatee karata hoon ki aeeson ke adhaeen raho, baran har aek ke jo is kam men paarishramee aur sahakarmee haain.
{16:17} aur maain staifanas aur fooratoonatus aur akhikus ke ane se anaandait hoo, kyonki unhon ne tumharee dhattee ko pooree kee haai.
{16:18} aur unhon ne meree aur tumharee atma ko chaain diya haai isaliye aeeson ko mano..
{16:19} aasiya kee kaleesiyaon kee or se tum ko namaskara akvaila aur pirsaka ka aur un ke ghar kee kaleesiya ko bhee tum ko prabhu men bahut bahut namaskara.
{16:20} sab bhaiyon ka tum ko namaskara: paavitra chumban se apas men namaskar karo..
{16:21} mujh paulus ka apane hath ka likha hua namaskara: yadi koi prabhu se praem n rakhe to vah srapit ho.
{16:22} hamara prabhu anevala haai.
{16:23} prabhu yeeshu maseeh ka anugrah tum par hota rahe.
{16:24} mera praem maseeh yeeshu men tum sab se rahe. ameena..
