{6:14} par aeesa n ho, ki maain aur kisee bat ka ghamand karoo, keval hamare prabhu yeeshu maseeh ke kroos ka jis ke dara sansar meree drashtti men aur maain sansar kee draashtti men kroos par chaddhaya gaya hoon.
{6:15} kyonaki n khtana, aur n khtanaraahit kuchh haai, parantu nai sraashtti.
{6:16} aur jitane is niyam par chalenge un par, aur parameshvar ke israael par, shaantai aur daya hotee rahe..
{6:17} age ko koi mujhe dukh n de, kyonaki maain yeeshu ke dagon ko apanee deh men liye firata hoon..
{6:18} he bhaiyo, hamare prabhu yeeshu maseeh ka anugrah tumharee atma ke sath rahe. ameena..
