{24:7} aur jab tum ne yahova kee dohai dee tab us ne tumhare aur misriyon ke beech men aandhiayara kar diya, aur un par samudra ko bahakar unako duba diya aur jo kuchh maain ne misr men kiya use tum logon ne apanee ankhon se dekha fir tum bahut din tak jangal men rahe.
{24:8} tab maain tum ko un aemoriyon ke desh men le aya, jo yaradan ke us par base the aur ve tum se lade aur maain ne unhen tumhare vash men kar diya, aur tum unake desh ke adhaikaree ho gaa, aur maain ne unako tumhare samhane se satyanash kar dala.
{24:9} fir moab ke raja sippor ka putra balak uttkar israael se lada aur tumhen shaap dene ke liye bor ke putra bilam ko bulava bheja,
{24:10} parantu maain ne bilam kee sunane ke liye naheen kee vah tum ko ashaeesh hee ashaeesh deta gaya is prakar maain ne tum ko usake hath se bachaya.
{24:11} tab tum yaradan par hokar yareeho ke pas aa, aur jab yareeho ke log, aur aemoree, paarijjee, kananee, hittee, girgashae, hibbee, aur yaboosee tum se lade, tab maain ne unhen tumhare vash men kar diya.
{24:12} aur maain ne tumhare age barron ko bheja, aur unhon ne aemoriyon ke donon rajaon ko tumhare samhane se bhga diya dekho, yah tumharee talavar va dhanush ka kam naheen hua.
{24:13} fir maain ne tumhen aeesa desh diya jis men tum ne parishram n kiya tha, aur aeese nagar bhee diae haain jinhen tum ne n basaya tha, aur tum un men base ho aur jin dakh aur jalapai ke bageechon ke fal tum khate ho unhen tum ne naheen lagaya tha.
{24:14} isaliye ab yahova ka bhy manakar usakee seva khrai aur sachchai se karo aur jin devataon kee seva tumhare purakha mahanad ke us par aur misr men karate the, unhen door karake yahova kee seva karo.
{24:15} aur yaadi yahova kee seva karanee tumhen buree lage, to aj chun lo ki tum kis kee seva karoge, chahe un devataon kee jinakee seva tumhare purakha mahanad ke us par karate the, aur chahe aemoriyon ke devataon kee seva karo jinake desh men tum rahate ho parantu maain to apane gharane samet yahova kee seva nit karoonga.
{24:16} tab logon ne uttr diya, yahova ko tyagakar doosare devataon kee seva karanee ham se door rahe
{24:17} kyonaki hamara parameshvar yahova vahee haai jo ham ko aur hamare purakhaon ko dasatv ke ghar, arthata misr desh se nikal le aya, aur hamare dekhte bade bade ashcharya karma kia, aur jis marga par aur jitanee jatiyon ke madhy men se ham chale ate the un men hamaree raksha kee
{24:18} aur hamare samhane se is desh men rahanevalee aemoree aadi sab jatiyon ko nikal diya haai isaaliye ham bhee yahova kee seva karenge, kyonaki hamara parameshvar vahee haai.
{24:19} yahoshoo ne logon se kaha, tum se yahova kee seva naheen ho sakatee kyonaki vah pavitra parameshvar haai vah jalan rakhnevala ishvar haai vah tumhare aparadha aur pap kshama n karega.
{24:20} yaadi tum yahova ko tyagakar paraae devataon kee seva karane lagoge, to yapi vah tumhara bhla karata aya haai taubhee vah firakar tumharee haani karega aur tumhara ant bhee kar dalega.
{24:21} logon ne yahoshoo se kaha, naheen ham yahova hee kee seva karenge.
{24:22} yahoshoo ne logon se kaha, tum ap hee apane sakshaee ho ki tum ne yahova kee seva karanee angeekar kar lee haai. unhon ne kaha, ha, ham sakshaee haain.
{24:23} yahoshoo ne kaha, apane beech paraae devataon ko door karake apana apana man israael ke parameshvar kee or lagao.
{24:24} logon ne yahoshoo se kaha, ham to apane parameshvar yahova hee kee seva karenge, aur usee kee bat manenge.
{24:25} tab yahoshoo ne usee din un logon se vacha bandhaai, aur shakem men unake liye vidhai aur niyam ttharaya..
{24:26} yah sara vrattant yahoshoo ne parameshvar kee vyavastha kee pustak men likh diya aur aek bada patthr chunakar vahan us banjavraksha ke tale khda kiya, jo yahova ke paavitra sthan men tha.
{24:27} tab yahoshoo ne sab logon se kaha, suno, yah patthr ham logon ka sakshaee rahega, kyonki jitane vachan yahova ne ham se kahen haain unhen is ne suna haai isaliye yah tumhara sakshaee rahega, aeesa n ho ki tum apane parameshvar se mukar jao.
{24:28} tab yahoshoo ne logon ko apane apane nij bhag par jane ke liye vida kiya..
{24:29} in baton ke bad yahova ka das, noon ka putra yahoshoo, aek sau das varsha ka hokar mar gaya.
{24:30} aur usako timnatserah me, jo aepraaim ke pahadee desh men gash nam pahad kee uttr alang par haai, usee ke bhag men mittee dee gai.
{24:31} aur yahoshoo ke jeevan bhr, aur jo vraddh log yahoshoo ke marane ke bad jeeavit rahe aur janate the ki yahova ne israael ke liye kaaise kaaise kam kiae the, unake bhee jeevan bhr israaelee yahova hee kee seva karate rahe.
{24:32} fir yoosufa kee haddiyan jinhen israaelee misr se le aae the ve shakem kee boomi ke us bhag men gadee gai, jise yakoob ne shakem ke pita hamor se aek sau chandee ke sikkon men mol liya tha isaliye vah yoosufa kee santan ka nij bhag ho gaya.
{24:33} aur haroon ka putra aeleeajar bhee mar gaya aur usako aepraaim ke pahadee desh men us pahadee par mittee dee gai, jo usake putra peenahas ke nam par gibatpeenahas kahalatee haai aur usako de dee gai thee..
