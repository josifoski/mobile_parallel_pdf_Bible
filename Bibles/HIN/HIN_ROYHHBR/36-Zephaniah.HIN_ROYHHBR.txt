{1:1} amon ke putra yahooda ke raja yoshaiyyah ke dinon me, sapanyah ke pas jo hijaakiyyah ke putra amaryah ka parapota aur gadalyah ka pota aur kooshaee ka putra tha, yahova ka yah vachan pahuncha:
{1:2} maain dharatee ke upar se sab ka ant kar doonga, yahova kee yahee vanee haai.
{1:3} maain manushy aur pashu donon ka ant kar doonga maain akash ke paakshiayon aur samudra kee machhaliyon ka, aur dushtton samet unakee rakhee hui ttokaron ke karan ka bhee ant kar doonga maain manushy jati ko bhee dharatee par se nash kar daloonga, yahova kee yahee vanee haai.
{1:4} maain yahooda par aur yarooshalem ke sab rahanevalon par hath uttaunga, aur is sthan men bal ke bache huon ko aur yajakon samet devataon ke pujariyon ke nam ko nash kar doonga.
{1:5} jo log apane apane ghar kee chht par akash ke gan ko dandvata karate huae yahova kee seva karane kee shapath khate haain
{1:6} aur jo yahova ke peechhe chalane se lautt gaae haai, aur jinhon ne n to yahova ko ddoonddha, aur n usakee khoj men lage, unako bhee maain satyanash kar daloonga..
{1:7} parameshvar yahova ke samhane shaant raho! kyonaki yahova ka din nikatt haai yahova ne yagya siddh kiya haai, aur apane pahunon ko pavitra kiya haai.
{1:8} aur yahova ke yagya ke din, maain hakimon aur rajakumaron ko aur jitane paradesh ke vasr pahina karate haai, unako bhee dand doonga.
{1:9} us din maain un sabhon ko dand doonga jo devaddhee ko langhate, aur apane svamee ke ghar ko upadrav aur chhl se bhr dete haain..
{1:10} yahova kee yah vanee haai, ki us din machhlee faattk ke pas chillahatt ka aur naye ttole mishnah men hahakar ka aur tteelon par bade dhamake ka shabd hoga.
{1:11} he maktesh ke rahanevalo, hay, hay, karo! kyonaki sab vyoparee mitt gaae jitane chandee se lade the, un sab ka nash ho gaya haai.
{1:12} us samay maain deepak liae huae yarooshalem men ddoonddh-ddhanddh karoonga, aur jo log dakhmadha ke talachhtt tatha maail ke saman baaitte huae man men kahate haain ki yahova n to bhla karega aur n bura, unako maain dand doonga.
{1:13} tab unakee dhan sampaati loottee jaaegee, aur unake ghar ujad honge ve ghar to banaaenge, parantu un men rahane n paaenge aur ve dakh kee bariyan lagaaenge, parantu un se dakhmadha n peene paaenge..
{1:14} rayahova ka bhyanak din nikatt haai, vah bahut veg se sameep chala ata haai yahova ke din ka shabd sun padta haai, vahan veer du: kh ke mare chillata haai.
{1:15} vah rosh ka din, vah ujad aur udhod ka din, vah badal aur kalee ghatta ka din hoga.
{1:16} vah gaddhvale nagaron aur unche gummatton ke virooddh narasinga foonkane aur lalakarane ka din hoga.
{1:17} maain manushyon ko sankatt men daloonga, aur ve andhaen kee nain chalenge, kyonki unhon ne yahova ke virooddh pap kiya haai unaka lohoo dhooli ke saman, aur unaka mans vishtta kee nain faenk diya jaaega.
{1:18} yahova ke rosh ke din me, n to chandee se unaka bachav hoga, aur n sone se kyonaki usake jalan kee ag se saree prathvee bhsm ho jaaegee vah prathvee ke sare rahanevalon ko ghabarakar unaka ant kar dalega..
{2:1} he nirlajj jati ke logo, ikatthe ho!
{2:2} is se paahile ki dand kee agyaa pooree ho aur bachav ka din boosee kee nain nikale, aur yahova ka bhdkata hua krodha tum par a pade, aur yahova ke krodha ka din tum par aa, tum ikatthe ho.
{2:3} he prathvee ke sab namra logo, he yahova ke niyam ke mananevalo, usako ddoonddhte raho dharma se ddoonddho, namrata se ddoonddho sambhv haai tum yahova ke krodha ke din men sharan pao.
{2:4} kyonaki ajja to nirjan aur ashkalon ujad ho jaaega ashadod ke nivasee dinadupaharee nikal diae jaaenge, aur aekron ukhada jaaega..
{2:5} samudrateer ke rahanevalon par haya karetee jaati par haya he kanan, he paalishtaiyon ke desha, yahova ka vachan tere virooddh haai aur maain tujh ko aeesa nash karoonga ki tujh men koi n bachega.
{2:6} aur usee samudrateer par charavahon ke ghar honge aur bhedshaalaon samet charai hogee.
{2:7} arthata vahee samudrateer yahooda ke gharane ke bache huon ko milegee, ve us par charaaenge ve ashkalon ke chhode huae gharon men sanjh ko lettenge, kyonki unaka parameshvar yahova unakee sudhai lekar unake bandhauon ko lautta le jaaega..
{2:8} moab ne jo meree praja kee namadharai aur ammoniyon ne jo usakee ninda karake usake desh kee seema par chaddhai kee, vah mere kanon tak pahunchee haai.
{2:9} is karan israael ke parameshvar, senaon ke yahova kee yah vanee haai, mere jeevan kee shapat, nishchay moab sadom ke saman, aur ammonee amora kee nain bichchoo pedon ke sthan aur namak kee khaniyan ho jaaenge, aur sadaaiv ujade rahenge. meree praja ke bache huae unako loottenge, aur meree jati ke shosh log unako apane bhag men paaenge.
{2:10} yah unake garva ka palatta hoga, kyonaki unhon ne senaon ke yahova kee praja kee namadharai kee, aur us par badai maree haai.
{2:11} yahova unako dravana dikhai dega, vah prathvee bhr ke devataon ko bookhon mar dalega, aur anyajaatiyon ke sab deepon ke nivasee apane apane sthan se usako dandvata karenge..
{2:12} he kooshaiyo, tum bhee meree talavar se mare jaoge.
{2:13} vah apana hath uttr disha kee or baddhakar ashshoor ko nash karega, aur neenave ko ujad kar jangal ke saman nirjal kar dega.
{2:14} usake beech men sab jaati ke vanapashu jhund ke jhund baaittenge usake khmbhon kee kangaaniyon par dhanesh aur sahee donon rat ko basera karenge aur usakee khaidkiyon men bola karenge usakee devaddhiyan soonee padee rahengee, aur devadar kee lakadee ughaaree jaegee.
{2:15} yah vahee nagaree haai, jo magan rahatee aur nidr baaittee rahatee thee, aushra sochatee thee ki maain hee hoo, aur mujhe chhod koi haai hee naheen. parantu ab yah ujad aur vanapashuuon ke baaittne ka sthan ban gaya haai, yahan tak ki jo koi isake pas hokar chale, vah talee bajaaega aur hath hilaaega.
{3:1} hay balava karanevalee aur ashuuddh aur andhor se bhree hui nagaree!
{3:2} us ne meree naheen sunee, us ne tadna se bhee naheen mana, us ne yahova par bhrosa naheen rakha, vah apane parameshvar ke sameep naheen ai..
{3:3} usake haakim garajanevale sinh tthare usake nyayee sanjh ko aher karanevale hundar haain jo bihan ke liye kuchh naheen chhodte.
{3:4} usake bhavishyadvkta vyarth bakanevale aur vishvasaghaatee haai, usake yajakon ne pavitrasthan ko ashuuddh kiya aur vyavastha men kheencha-khanch kee haai.

{3:6} maain ne anyajaatiyon ko yahan tak nash kiya, ki unake konevale gummatt ujad gaae maain ne unakee sadkon ko yahan tak soonee kiya, ki koi un par naheen chalata unake nagar yahan tak nash huae ki un men koi manushy varan koi bhee praanee naheen raha.

{3:8} is karan yahova kee yah vanee haai, ki jab tak maain nash karane ko n utoo, tab tak tum meree batt johate raho. maain ne yah ttana haai ki jati-jati ke aur rajya-rajy ke logon ko maain ikattha karoo, ki un par apane krodha kee ag pooree reeati se bhdkaun kyonaki saree prathvee meree jalan kee ag se bhsm ho jaaegee..
{3:9} aur us samay maain desha-desh ke logon se aek nai aur shuuddh bhasha bulavaunga, ki ve sab ke sab yahova se praarthna kare, aur aek man se kandho se kandha milaae huae usakee seva karen.
{3:10} meree titara-abitar kee hui praja mujh se binatee karatee hui meree bhentt banakar aaegee..
{3:11} us din, too apane sab bade se bade kamon se jinhen karake too mujh se fir gai thee, fir lajjait n hogee. us samay maain tere beech se sab foole huae ghamaandiyon ko door karoonga, aur too mere pavitra parvat par fir kabhee aabhaiman n karegee.
