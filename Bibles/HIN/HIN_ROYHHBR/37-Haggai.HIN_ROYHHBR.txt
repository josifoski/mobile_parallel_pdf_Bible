{1:1} dara raja ke doosare varsha ke chhttven maheene ke paahile din, yahova ka yah vachan, haggaai bhavishyadvkta ke dara, shaalateeael ke putra jaroobbabel ke pas, jo yahooda ka adhaipaati tha, aur yahosadak ke putra yahoshoo mahayajak ke pas pahuncha:
{1:2} senaon ka yahova yon kahata haai, ye log kahate haain ki yahova ka bhvan banane ka samay naheen aya haai.
{1:3} fir yahova ka yah vachan haggaai bhavishyadvkta ke dara pahuncha,
{1:4} kya tumhare liye apane chhtavale gharon men rahane ka samay haai, jab ki yah bhvan ujad pada haai?
{1:5} isaliye ab senaon ka yahova yon kahata haai, apanee apanee chala-chalan par dhyan karo.
{1:6} tum ne bahut boya parantu thoda katta tum khate ho, parantu pett naheen bhrata tum peete ho, parantu pyas naheen bujhtee tum kapade paahinate ho, parantu garamate naheen aur jo majadooree kamata haai, vah apanee majadooree kee kamai ko chhedavalee thailee men rakhta haai..
{1:7} senao ka yahova tum se yon kahata he, apane apane chalachalan par socho.
{1:8} pahad par chaddh jao aur lakadee le ao aur is bhvan ko banao aur maain usako dekhkar prasann hoonga, aur meree mahima hogee, yahova ka yahee vachan haai.
{1:9} tum ne bahut upaj kee asha rakhee, parantu dekho thedee hee haai aur jab tum use ghar le aa, tab maain ne usako uda diya. senaon ke yahova kee yah vanee haai, aeesa kyon hua? kya isaliye nahee, ki mera bhvan ujad pada haai aur tum men se pratyek apane apane ghar ko dauda chala jata haai?
{1:10} is karan akash se os girana aur prathvee se ann upajana donon band haain.
{1:11} aur meree agyaa se prathvee aur pahadon par, aur ann aur naye dakhmadha par aur taje tel par, aur jo kuchh boomi se upajata haai us par, aur manushyon aur gharaailoo pashuuon par, aur unake parishram kee saree kamai par bhee akal pada haai..
{1:12} tab shaalateeael ke putra jaroobbabel aur yahosadak ke putra yahoshoo mahayajak ne sab bache huae logon samet apane paramashevar yahova kee bat manee aur jo vachan unake parameshvar yahova ne un se kahane ke liye haggaai bhavishyadvkta ko bhej diya tha, use unhon ne man liya aur logon ne yahova ka bhy mana.
{1:13} tab yahova ke doot haggaai ne yahova se agyaa pakar un logon se yah kaha, yahova kee yah vanee haai, maain tumhare sang hoon.
{1:14} aur yahova ne shaalateeael ke putra jaroobbabel ko jo yahooda ka adhaipaati tha, aur yahosadak ke putra yahoshoo mahayajak ko, aur sab bache huae logon ke man ko ubhar ka utsah se bhr diya ki ve akar apane parameshvar, senaon ke yahova ke bhvan ko banane men lag jaaen.
{1:15} yah dara raja ke doosare varsha ke chhttven maheene ke chaubeesaven din hua..
{2:1} fir sataven maheene ke ikkeesaven din ko yahova ka yah vachan haggaai bhavishyadvkta ke pas pahuncha,
{2:2} shaalateeael ke putra yahooda ke adhaipaati jaroobbabel, aur yahosadak ke putra yahoshoo mahayajak aur sab bache huae logon se yah bat kah,
{2:3} tum men se kaun haai, jis ne is bhvan kee paahilee maahima dekhee haai? ab tum ise kaaisee dasha men dekhte ho? kya yah sach naheen ki yah tumharee draashtti men us pahile kee apeksha kuchh bhee achchha naheen haai?
{2:4} taubhee, ab yahova kee yah vanee haai, he jaroobbabel, hiyav bandha aur he yahosadak ke putra yahoshoo mahayajak, hiyav bandha aur yahova kee yah bhee vanee haai ki he desh ke sab logo hiyav bandhakar kam karo, kyonaki maain tumhare sang hoo, senaon ke yahova kee yahee vanee haai.
{2:5} tumhare misr se nikalane ke samay jo vacha maain ne tum se bandhaee thee, usee vacha ke anusar mera atma tumhare beech men bana haai isaaliye tum mat dro.
{2:6} kyonaki senaon ka yahova yon kahata haai, ab thodee hee der bakee haai ki maain akash aur prathvee aur samudra aur sthl sab ko kampait karoonga.
{2:7} aushra maai. saree jaatiyon ko kampakapaunga, aur saree jaatiyon kee manabhavanee vastuaen aaengee aur maain is bhvan ko apanee mahima ke tej se bhr doonga, senaon ke yahova ka yahee vachan haai.
{2:8} chandee to meree haai, aur sona bhee mera hee haai, senaon ke yahova kee yahee vanee haai.
{2:9} is bhvan kee pichhlee mahima isakee paahilee maahima se badee hogee, senaon ke yahova ka yahee vachan haai, aur is sthan men maain shaantai doonga, senaon ke yahova kee yahee vanee haai..
{2:10} dara ke doosare varsha ke nauven maheene ke chaubeesave din ko, yahova ka yah vachan haggaai bhavishyadvkta ke pas pahuncha,
{2:11} senaon ka yahova yon kahata haai: yajakon se is bat kee vyavastha pooch,
{2:12} yaadi koi apane vasr ke anchal se rottee va pakaae huae bhojan va dakhmadha va tel va kisee prakar ke bhojan ko chhua, to kya vah bhojan pavitra ttharega? yajakon ne uttr diya, naheen.
{2:13} fir haggaai ne poochha, yadi koi jan manushy kee loth ke karan ashuuddh hokar aeesee kisee vastu ko chhua, to kya vah ashuuddh ttharegee? yajakon ne uttr diya, han ashuuddh ttharegee.
{2:14} fir haggaai ne kaha, yahova kee yahee vanee haai, ki meree draashtti men yah praja aur yah jaati vaaisee hee haai, aur inake sab kam bhee vaaise haain aur jo kuchh ve vahan chaddhate haai, vah bhee ashuuddh haai
{2:15} ab socha-avichar karo ki aj se pahile arthata jab yahova ke maandair men patthr par patthr rakha hee naheen gaya tha,
{2:16} un dinon men jab koi ann ke bees napuon kee asha se jata, tab das hee pata tha, aur jab koi dakhras ke kund ke pas is asha se jata ki pachas bartan bhr nikale, tab bees hee nikalate the.
{2:17} maain ne tumharee saree khetee ko loo aur garooi aur olon se mara, taubhee tum meree or n fire, yahova kee yahee vanee haai.
{2:18} ab socha-avichar karo, ki aj se pahile arthata jis din yahova ke mandair kee nev dalee gai, us din se lekar nauven maheene ke isee chaubeesaven din tak kya dasha thee? isaka socha-vichar karo.
{2:19} kya ab tak beech khtte men haai? ab tak dakhlata aur anjeer aur anar aur jalapai ke vraksha naheen fale, parantu aj ke din se maain tum ko ashaeesh deta rahoonga..
{2:20} usee maheene ke chaubeesaven din ko doosaree bar yahova ka yah vachan haggaai ke pas pahuncha, yahooda ke adhaipaati jaroobbabel se yon kaha:
{2:21} maain akash aur prathvee donon ko kampaunga,
{2:22} aur maain rajya-rajy kee gaee ko ulatt doonga maain anyajatiyon ke rajya-rajy ka bal tooonga, aur rathon ko chaddhvaaiyon samet ulatt doonga aur ghaedon samet savar aek doosare kee talavar se girenge.
{2:23} senaon ke yahova kee yahee vanee haai, us din, he shaalateeael ke putra mere das jaroobbabel, maain tujhe lekar angoottee ke saman rakoonga, yahova kee yahee vanee haai kyonki maain ne tujhee ko chun liya haai, senaon ke yahova kee yahee vanee haai..
