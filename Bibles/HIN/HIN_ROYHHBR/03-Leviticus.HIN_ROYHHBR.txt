{27:1} fir yahova ne moosa se kaha,
{27:2} israaeliyon se yah kah, ki jab koi vishosh sankalp mane, to sankalp kiae huae praanee tere ttharane ke anusar yahova ke honge
{27:3} isaliye yaadi vah bees varsha va us se aadhaik aur satt varsha se kam avastha ka puroosh ho, to usake liye paavitrasthan ke shokel ke anusar pachas shokel ka roopaya tthare.
{27:4} aur yaadi vah sree ho, to tees shokel tthare.
{27:5} fir yadi usakee avastha panch varsha va usase aadhaik aur bees varsha se kam kee ho, to ladke ke liye to bees shokel, aur ladkee ke liye das shokel tthare.

{27:7} fir yadi usakee avastha satt varsha kee va us se adhaik ho, aur vah puroosh ho to usake liye pandrah shokel, aur sree ho to das shokel tthare.
