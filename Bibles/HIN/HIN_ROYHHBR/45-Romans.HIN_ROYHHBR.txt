{16:5} aur us kaleesiya ko bhee namaskar jo un ke ghar men haai. mere piry ipaaianitus ko jo maseeh ke liye aasiya ka paahila fal haai, namaskara.
{16:6} maariyam ko jis ne tumhare liye bahut parishram kiya, namaskara.
{16:7} andrauneekus aur yooniyas ko jo mere kuttmbee haai, aur mere sath kaaid huae the, aur praeriton men namee haai, aur mujh se pahile maseeh men huae the, namaskara.
{16:8} ampaaliyatus ko, jo prabhu men mera piry haai, namaskara.
{16:9} urabanus ko, jo maseeh men hamara sahakarmee haai, aur mere piry istakhus ko namaskara.
{16:10} aapilles ko jo maseeh men khra nikala, namaskara. aristubulus ke gharane ko namaskara.
{16:11} mere kuttumbee herodiyon ko namaskara. narakiyus ke gharane ke jo log prabhu men haai, un ko namaskara.
{16:12} troofaaina aur troofaosa ko jo prabhu men paarishram karatee haai, namaskara. pirya piraasis ko jis ne prabhu men bahut parishram kiya, namaskara.
{16:13} roofaus ko jo prabhu men chuna hua haai, aur us kee mata ko jo meree bhee haai, donon ko namaskara.
{16:14} asukritus aur filagon aur himas or patraubas aur himans aur un ke sath ke bhaiyon ko namaskara.
{16:15} filulugus aur yooliya aur neryus aur us kee bahin, aur ulumpas aur un ke sath ke sab pavitra chumban se namaskar karo: tum ko maseeh kee saree kaleesiyaon kee or se namaskara..
{16:16} apas men pavitra chumban se namaskar karo tum ko maseeh kee saree kaleesyaiaon kee or se namaskara.
{16:17} ab he bhaiyo, maain tum se binatee karata hoo, ki jo log us shaiksha ke vipareet jo tum ne pai haai, foott padne, aur ttokar khane ke karan hote haai, unhen tad liya karo aur un se door raho.
{16:18} kyonaki aeese log hamare prabhu maseeh kee nahee, parantu apane pett kee seva karate haai aur chikanee chumadee baton se seedho sade man ke logon ko bahaka dete haain.
{16:19} tumhare agyaa manane kee charcha sab logon men faail gai haai isaaliye maain tumhare vishay men anand karata hoon parantu maain yah chahata hoo, ki tum bhlai ke liye buaddhmaian, parantu burai ke liye bhole bane raho.
{16:20} shaantai ka parameshvar shautan ko tumhare panvon se shaeegha kuchalava dega.. hamare prabhu yeeshu maseeh ka anugrah tum par hota rahe.
{16:21} teemuthaiyus mere sahakarmee ka, aur lookiyus aur yason aur sosipatraus mere kuttuambaiyon ka, tum ko namaskara.
{16:22} mujh patraee ke likhnevale tiraatiyus ka prabhu men tum ko namaskara.
{16:23} gayus ka jo meree aur kaleesiya ka pahunai karanevala haai usaka tumhen namaskara: irastus jo nagar ka bhndaree haai, aur bhai kvaratus ka, tum ko namaskara..
{16:24} 
{16:25} ab jo tum ko mere susamachar arthata yeeshu maseeh ke vishay ke prachar ke anusar sthiar kar sakata haai, us bhed ke prakash ke anusar jo sanatan se chhpai raha.
{16:26} parantu ab pragatt hokar sanatan parameshvar kee agyaa se bhavishyadvktaon kee pustakon ke dara sab jatiyon ko bataya gaya haai, ki ve vishvas se agyaa mananevale ho jaaen.
{16:27} usee advait buddhmaian parameshvar kee yeeshu maseeh ke dara yuganuyug maahima hotee rahe. ameena..
