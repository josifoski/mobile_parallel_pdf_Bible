{1:26} fir parameshvar ne kaha, ham manushy ko apane svaroop ke anusar apanee samanata men banaaen aur ve samudr kee machhliyo, aur akash ke pakshiayo, aur ghareloo pashuuo, aur saree prathvee par, aur sab renganevale jantuon par jo prathvee par rengate haai, adhaikar rakhen.
