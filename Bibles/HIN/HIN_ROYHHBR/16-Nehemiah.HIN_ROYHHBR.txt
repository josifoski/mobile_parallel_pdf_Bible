{13:22} tab maain ne leviyon ko agyaa dee, ki apane apane ko shuuddh karake faattkon kee rakhvalee karane ke liye aya karo, taaki vishraamadin pavitra mana jaae. he mere parameshvar! mere hit ke liye yah bhee smaran rakh aur apanee badee karuna ke anusar mujh par taras kha.
{13:23} fir unheen dinon men mujh ko aeese yahoodee dikhai pade, jinhon ne ashadodee, ammonee aur moabee striayan byah lee theen.
{13:24} aur unake ladkebalon kee adhaee bolee ashadodee thee, aur ve yahoodee bolee n bol sakate the, donon jati kee bolee bolate the.
{13:25} tab maain ne unako dantta aur kosa, aur un men se kitanon ko pittva diya aur unake bal nuchavaae aur unako parameshvar kee yah shapath khailai, ki ham apanee bettyaian unake betton ke sath byah men n denge aur n apane liye va apane betton ke liye unakee beettyaian byah men lenge.
{13:26} kya israaael ka raja sulaaiman isee prakar ke pap men n fansa th? bahuteree jaatiyon men usake tuly koi raja naheen hua, aur vah apane parameshvar ka piry bhee tha, aur parameshvar ne use sare israaael ke upar raja niyukt kiya parantu usako bhee anyajaati striayon ne pap men fansaya.
{13:27} to kya ham tumharee sunakar, aeesee badee burai karen ki anyajaati kee striayan byah kar apane parameshvar ke viruddh pap karen?
{13:28} aur aelyashaeeb mahayajak ke putra yoyada ka aek putra, horonee samballat ka damad tha, isaliye maain ne usako apane pas se bhga diya.
{13:29} he mere parameshvar unakee hani ke liye yajakapad aur yajakon or leviyon kee vacha ka toda jana smaran rakh.
{13:30} is prakar maain ne unako sab anyajatiyon se shuuddh kiya, aur aek aek yajak aur leveey kee baree aur kam tthara diya.
{13:31} fir maain ne lakadee kee bhentt le ane ke vishosh samay tthara dia, aur paahilee paahilee upaj ke dene ka prabandha bhee kiya. he mere parameshvar! mere hit ke liye mujhe smaran kara.
