{1:1} paulus aur silavanus aur teemuthaiyus kee or se thaissaluneekiyon kee kaleesiya ke nam, jo hamare pita parameshvar aur prabhu yeeshu maseeh men haai..
{1:2} hamare pita parameshvar aur prabhu yeeshu maseeh men tumhen anugrah aur shaaantai milatee rahe..
{1:3} he bhaiyo, tumhare vishay men hamen har samay parameshvar ka dhanyavad karana chahia, aur yah uchit bhee haai isaaliye ki tumhara vishvas bahut baddhta jata haai, aur tum sab ko praem apas men bahut hee hota jata haai.
{1:4} yahan tak ki ham ap parameshvar kee kaleeasiya men tumhare vishay men ghamand karate haai, ki jitane upadrav aur klesh tum sahate ho, un sab men tumhara dhaeeraj aur vishvas pragatt hota haai.
{1:5} yah parameshvar ke sachche nyay ka spashtt praman haai ki tum parameshvar ke rajy ke yogy ttharo, jis ke liye tum dukh bhee uttate ho.
{1:6} kyonaki parameshvar ke nikatt yah nyay haai, ki jo tumhen klesh dete haai, unhen badale men klesh de.
{1:7} aur tumhen jo klesh pate ho, hamare sath chaain de us samay jab ki prabhu yeeshu apane samarthee dooton ke sat, dhadhakatee hui ag men svarga se pragatt hoga.
{1:8} aur jo parameshvar ko naheen pahachanate, aur hamare prabhu yeeshu ke susamachar ko naheen manate un se palatta lega.
{1:9} ve prabhu ke samhane se, aur usakee shaaktai ke tej se door hokar anant vinash ka dand paaenge.
{1:10} yah us din hoga, jab vah apane paavitra logon men mahima pane, aur sab vishvas karanevalon men ashcharya ka karan hone ko aaega kyonaki tum ne hamaree gavahee kee prateeati kee.
{1:11} isee liye ham sada tumhare nimitt praarthna bhee karate haai, ki hamara parameshvar tumhen is bulahatt ke yogy samajhe, aur bhlai kee har aek ichchha, aur vishvas ke har aek kam ko samarth sahit poora kare.
{1:12} ki hamare parameshvar aur prabhu yeeshu maseeh ke anugrah ke anusar hamare prabhu yeeshu ka nam tum men maahima paa, aur tum us men..
{2:1} he bhaiyo, tum apane prabhu yeeshu maseeh ke ane, aur usake pas apane ikatthe hone ke vishay men tum se binatee karate haain.
{2:2} ki kisee atma, ya vachan, ya patraee ke dara jo ki manon hamaree or se ko, yah samajhkar ki prabhu ka din a pahuncha haai, tumhara man achanak aasthiar n ho jaae aur n tum ghabarao.
{2:3} kisee reeati se kisee ke dhaekhe men n ana kyonaki vah din n aaega, jab tak dharma ka tyag n ho le, aur vah pap ka puroosh arthata vinash ka putra pragatt n ho.
{2:4} jo virodha karata haai, aur har aek se jo parameshvar, ya poojy kahalata haai, apane ap ko bada ttharata haai, yahan tak ki vah parameshvar ke mandair men baaittkar apane ap ko parameshvar pragatt karata haai.
{2:5} kya tumhen smaran nahee, ki jab maain tumhare yahan tha, to tum se ye baten kaha karata tha?
{2:6} aur ab tum us vastu ko janate ho, jo use rok rahee haai, ki vah apane hee samay men pragatt ho.
{2:7} kyonaki adharma ka bhed ab bhee kayrya karata jata haai, par abhee aek rokanevala haai, aur jab tak vah door n ho jaa, vah roke rahega.
{2:8} tab vah adharmee pragatt hoga, jise prabhu yeeshu apane munh ko faook se mar dalega, aur apane agaman ke tej se bhsm karega.
{2:9} us adharmee ka ana shautan ke kayrya ke anusar sab prakar kee joottee samarth, aur chinh, aur adabhut kam ke sath.
{2:10} aur nash honevalon ke liye adharma ke sab prakar ke dhaekhe ke sath hoga kyonaki unhonne saty ke praem ko grahan naheen kiya jis se un ka uddhar hota.
{2:11} aur isee karan parameshvar un men aek bhttka denevalee samarth ko bhejega taki ve joott kee prateeti karen.
{2:12} aur jitane log saty kee prateeti naheen karate, varan adharma se prasann hote haai, sab dand paaen..
{2:13} par he bhaiyo, aur prabhu ke piry logo chahiye ki ham tumhare vishay men sada parameshvar ka dhanyavad karate rahe, ki parameshvar ne adi se tumhen chun liya ki atma ke dara paavitra banakar, aur saty kee prateeti karake uddhar pao.
{2:14} jis ke liye us ne tumhen hamare susamachar ke dara bulaya, ki tum hamare prabhu yeeshu maseeh kee maahima ko praapt karo.
{2:15} isaliye, ye bhaiyo, sthiara aur jo jo baten tum ne kya vachan, kya patraee ke dara ham se seekhee haai, unhen thame raho..
{2:16} hamara prabhu yeeshu maseeh ap hee, aur hamara pita parameshvar jis ne ham se praem rakha, aur anugrah se anant shaaantai aur uttm asha dee haai.
{2:17} tumhare manon men shaantai de, aur tumhen har aek achchhe kam, aur vachan men draddh kare..
{3:1} nidan, he bhaiyo, hamare liye praarthna kiya karo, ki prabhu ka vachan aeesa shaeegha faaile, aur maahima paa, jaaisa tum men hua.
{3:2} aur ham tteddhe aur dushtt manushyon se bache rahen kyonaki har aek men vishvas naheen..
{3:3} parantu prabhu sachcha haai vah tumhen draddhta se sthiar karega: aur us dushtt se suraakshiat rakhega.
{3:4} aur hamen prabhu men tumhare upar bhrosa haai, ki jo jo agyaa ham tumhen dete haai, unhen tum manate ho, aur manate bhee rahoge.
{3:5} parameshvar ke praem aur maseeh ke dhaeeraj kee or prabhu tumhare man kee aguvai kare..
{3:6} he bhaiyo, ham tumhen apane prabhu yeeshu maseeh ke nam se agyaa dete haain ki har aek aeese bhai se alag raho, jo anuachit chal chalata, aur jo shaiksha us ne ham se pai usake anusar naheen karata.
{3:7} kyonaki tum ap janate ho, ki kis reeti se hamaree see chal chalanee chahiae kyonaki ham tumhare beech men anuchit chal n chale.
{3:8} aur kisee kee rottee sent men n khai par paarishram aur kashtt se rat din kam dhandha karate the, ki tum men se kisee par bhar n ho.
{3:9} yah nahee, ki hamen aadhaikar naheen par isaliye ki apane ap ko tumhare liye adarsha ttharaae, ki tum hamaree see chal chalo.
{3:10} aur jab ham tumhare yahan the, tab bhee yah agyaa tumhen dete the, ki yadi koi kam karana n chahe, to khane bhee n paae.
{3:11} ham sunate haai, ki kitane log tumhare beech men anuchit chal chalate haain aur kuchh kam naheen karate, par auron ke kam men hath dala karate haain.
{3:12} aeeson ko ham prabhu yeeshu maseeh men agyaa dete aur samajhate haai, ki chupachap kam karake apanee hee rottee khaya karen.
{3:13} aur tum, he bhaiyo, bhlai karane men hiyav n chhodo.
{3:14} yaadi koi hamaree is patraee kee bat ko n mane, to us par drashtti rakho aur us kee sangaati n karo, jis se vah lajjait ho
{3:15} taubhee use baairee man samajho par bhai janakar chitao..
{3:16} ab prabhu jo shaaantai ka sota haai ap hee tumhen sada aur har prakar se shaantai de: prabhu tum sab ke sath rahe..
{3:17} maain paulus apane hath se namaskar likhta hoon: har patraee men mera yahee chinh haai: maain isee prakar se likhta hoon.
{3:18} hamare prabhu yeeshu maseeh ka anugrah tum sab par hota rahe..
