{1:1} obadhah ka darshana.. ham logon ne yahova kee or se samachar suna haai, aur aek doot anyajaatiyon men yah kahane ko bheja gaya haai:
{1:2} utto! ham us se ladne ko utten! maain tujhe jaatiyon men chhotta kar doonga, too bahut tuchchh gina jaega.
{1:3} he pahadon kee dararon men basanevale, he unche sthan men rahanevale, tere abhaiman ne tujhe dhaekha diya haai too man men kahata haai,
{1:4} kaun mujhe boomi par utar dega? parantu chahe too ukab kee nain uncha udta ho, varan taragan ke beech apana ghaensala banaae ho, taubhee maain tujhe vahan se neeche giraunga, yahova kee yahee vanee haai..
{1:5} yaadi chora-dakoo rat ko tere pas ate, hay, too kaaise mitta diya gaya haai! to kya ve churaae huae dhan se trapt hokar chale n jate? aur yaadi dakh ke todnevale tere pas ate, to kya ve kaheen kaheen dakh n chhod jate?
{1:6} parantu aesav ka dhan kaaise khojakar lootta gaya haai, usaka gupt dhan kaaise pata laga lagakar nikala gaya haai!
{1:7} jitanon ne tujh se vacha bandhaee thee, un sabhon ne tujhe sivane tak ddhkel diya haai jo log tujh se mel rakhte the, ve tujh ko dhaeka dekar tujh par prabal huae haain ve teree rottee khate haai, ve tere liye fanda lagate haain-- us men kuchh samajh naheen haai.
{1:8} yahova kee yah vanee haai, kya maain us samay aedom men se buaddhmaianon ko, aur aesav ke pahad men se chaturai ko nash n karoonga?
{1:9} aur he teman, tere shooraveeron ka man kachcha n ho jaaega, aur yon aesav ke pahad par ka har aek puroosh ghaat hokar nash ho jaaega.
{1:10} he aesav, aek upadrav ke karan jo too ne apane bhai yakoob par kiya, too lajja se ddhnpega aur sada ke liye nash ho jaaega.
{1:11} jis din paradeshaee log usakee dhan sampaati chheenakar le gaa, aur birane logon ne usake faattkon se ghausakar yarooshalem par chitthee dalee, us din too bhee un men se aek tha.
{1:12} parantu tujhe uchit n tha ki too apane bhai ke din me, arthata usakee vipaati ke din men usakee or dekhta rahata, aur yahoodiyon ke nash hone ke din unake upar anand karata, aur unake sankatt ke din bada bol bolata.
{1:13} tujhe uchit n tha ki meree praja kee vipaati ke din too usake faattk se ghausata, aur usakee vipaati ke din usakee dhan sampati par hath lagata.
{1:14} tujhe uchit n tha ki tiramuhane par usake bhaganevalon ko mar dalane ke liye khda hota, aur sankatt ke din usake bache huon ko pakadata..
{1:15} kyonaki saree anyajatiyon par yahova ke din ka ana nikatt haai. jaaisa tu ne kiya haai, vaaisa hee tujh se bhee kiya jaaega, tera vyavahar lauttkar tere hee sir par padega.
{1:16} jis prakar too ne mere pavitra parvat par piya, usee prakar se saree anyajaatiyan lagatar peetee rahengee, varan ve sudka-sudkakar peeaengee, aur aesee ho jaaengee jaaisee kabhee hui hee naheen.
{1:17} parantu us samay siyyon parvat par bache huae log rahenge, or vah pavitrasthan ttharega aur yakoob ka gharana apane nij bhagon ka aadhaikaree hoga.
{1:18} tab yakoob ka gharana ag, aur yoosufa ka gharana lau, aur aesav ka gharana koonttee banega aur ve un men ag lagakar unako bhsm karenge, aur aesav ke gharane ka koi n bachega kyonaki yahova hee ne aeesa kaha haai..
{1:19} daakkhian desh ke log aesav ke pahad ke aadhaikaree ho jaaenge, aur neeche ke desh ke log palishtaiyon ke aadhaikaree honge aur yahoodee, aepraaim aur samariya ke dihat ko apane bhag men kar lenge, aur binyameen gilad ka aadhaikaree hoga.
{1:20} israaeliyon ke us dal men se jo lag bandhauai men jakar kananiyon ke beech sarapat tak rahate haai, aur yarooshaleamiyon men se jo log bandhauai men jakar saparad men rahate haai, ve sab dakkhian desh ke nagaron ke adhaikaree ho jaaenge.
{1:21} aur uddhar karanevale aesav ke pahad ka nyay karane ke liye siyyon parvat par chaddh aaenge, aur rajy yahova hee ka ho jaaega..
