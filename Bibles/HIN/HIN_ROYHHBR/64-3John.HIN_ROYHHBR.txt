{1:1} mujh praacheen kee or se us piry gayus ke nam, jis se maain sachcha praem rakhta hoon..
{1:2} he piry, meree yah praarthna haai ki jaaise too atmaik unnati kar raha haai, vaaise hee too sab baton me unnaati kare, aur bhla changa rahe.
{1:3} kyonaki jab bhaiyon ne akar, tere us saty kee gavahee dee, jis par too sachamuch chalata haai, to maain bahut hee anandait hua.
{1:4} mujhe is se baddhkar aur koi anand nahee, ki maain sunoo, ki mere ladke-bale saty par chalate haain.
{1:5} he piry, jo kuchh too un bhaiyon ke sath karata haai, jo paradeshaee bhee haai, use vishvasee kee nai karata haai.
{1:6} unhon ne mandlee ke samhane tere praem kee gavahee dee thee: yaadi too unhen us prakar vida karega jis prakar parameshvar ke logon ke liye uchit haai to achchha karega.
{1:7} kyonaki ve us nam ke liye nikale haai, aur anyajaatiyon se kuchh naheen lete.
{1:8} isaliye aeeson ka svagat karana chahia, jis se ham bhee saty ke paksha men un ke sahakarmee hon..
{1:9} maain ne mandlee ko kuchh likha tha par diyuatraifaes jo un men bada banana chahata haai, hamen grahan naheen karata.
{1:10} so jab maain aunga, to usake kamon kee jo vah kar raha haai suadhai dilaunga, ki vah hamare vishay men buree buree baten bakata haai aur is par bhee santosh n karake ap hee bhaiyon ko grahan naheen karata, aur unhen jo grahan karana chahate haai, mana karata haai: aur mandlee se nikal deta haai.
{1:11} he piry, burai ke nahee, par bhlai ke anuyayee ho, jo bhlai karata haai, vah parameshvar kee or se haai par jo burai karata haai, us ne parameshvar ko naheen dekha.
{1:12} demetraiyus ke vishay men sab ne baran saty ne bhee ap hee gavahee dee: aushra ham bhee gavahee dete haai, aur too janata haai, ki hamaree gavahee sachcha haai..
{1:13} mujhe tujh ko bahut kuchh likhna to tha par siyahee aur kalam se likhna naheen chahata.
{1:14} par mujhe asha haai ki tujh se shaeegha bhentt karoonga: tab ham amhane samhane batacheet karenge: tujhe shaaantai milatee rahe. yahan ke mitra tujhe namaskar karate haain: vahan ke mitraen ke nam le lekar namaskar kah dena..
