{36:8} aur israaeliyon ke kisee gotra men kisee kee bettee ho jo bhag panevalee ho, vah apane hee moolapuroosh ke gotra ke kisee puroosh se byahee jaa, isaliye ki israaelee apane apane moolapuroosh ke bhag ke aadhaikaree rahen.
{36:9} kisee gotra ka bhag doosare gotra ke bhag men milane n paaen israaeliyon ke aek aek gotra ke log apane apane bhag par bane rahen.
{36:10} yahova kee agyaa ke anusar jo us ne moosa ko dee salofaad kee bettyaien ne kiya.
{36:11} arthata mahala, tirsa, hogla, milaka, aur noa, jo salofaad kee beattyaian thee, unhon ne apane chachere bhaiyon se byah kiya.
{36:12} ve yoosufa ke putra manashsho ke vansh ke kulon men byahee gai, aur unaka bhag unake moolapuroosh ke kul ke gotra ke aadhaikar men bana raha..
{36:13} jo agyaaaen aur niyam yahova ne moab ke araba men yareeho ke pas kee yaradan nadee ke teer par moosa ke dara israaeliyon ko diae ve ye hee haain..
