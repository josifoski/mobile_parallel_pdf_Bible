{1:1} parameshvar ke aur prabhu yeeshu maseeh ke das yakoob kee or se un barahon gotraen ko jo tittr bittr hokar rahate haain namaskar pahunche..

{1:3} to ise poore anand kee bat samajho, yah janakar, ki tumhare vishvas ke parakhe jane se dhaeeraj utpann hota haai.
{1:4} par dhaeeraj ko apana poora kam karane do, ki tum poore aur siddh ho jao aur tum men kisee bat kee ghattee n rahe..
{1:5} par yaadi tum men se kisee ko buaddh ikee ghattee ho, to parameshvar se mange, jo bina ulahana diae sab ko udarata se deta haai aur us ko dee jaaegee.
{1:6} par vishvas se mange, aur kuchh sandeh n kare kyonki sandeh karanevala samudra kee lahar ke saman haai jo hava se bahatee aur uchhlatee haai.
{1:7} aeesa manushy yah n samajhe, ki mujhe prabhu se kuchh milega.
{1:8} vah vyaktai duchitta haai, aur apanee saree baton men chanchal haai..
{1:9} deen bhai apane unche pad par ghamand kare.
{1:10} aur dhanavan apanee neech dasha para: kyonaki vah ghaas ke fool kee nai jata rahega.
{1:11} kyonaki soorya uday hote hee kadee dhoop padtee haai aur ghaas ko sukha detee haai, aur usaka fool jhd jata haai, aur us kee shaebha jatee rahatee haai usee prakar dhanavan bhee apane marga par chalate chalate dhool men mil jaaega.
{1:12} dhany haai vah manushy, jo pareeksha men sthiar rahata haai kyonki vah khra nikalakar jeevan ka vah mukutt paaega, jis kee pratigyaa prabhu ne apane praem karanevalon ko dee haai.
{1:13} jab kisee hee pareeksha ho, to vah yah n kahe, ki meree pareeksha parameshvar kee or se hotee haai kyonaki n to buree baton se parameshvar kee pareeksha ho sakatee haai, aur n vahee kisee kee pareeksha ap karata haai.
{1:14} parantu pratyek vyaaktai apanee hee abhailasha men khainchakar, aur fansakar pareeksha men padta haai.
{1:15} fir abhailasha garbhvatee hokar pap ko janata haai aur pap baddh jata haai to mratyu ko utpann karata haai.
{1:16} he mere piry bhaiyo, dhaekha n khao.
{1:17} kyonaki har aek achchha varadan aur har aek uttm dan upar hee se haai, aur jyotiyon ke pita kee or se milata haai, jis men n to koi parivartan ho sakata haai, or n adal badal ke karan us par chhaya padtee haai.
{1:18} us ne apanee hee ichchha se hamen saty ke vachan ke dara utpann kiya, taaki ham us kee srashtti kee hui vastuon men se ek prakar ke prathm fal hon..
{1:19} he mere piry bhaiyo, yah bat tum janate ho: isaliye har aek manushy sunane ke liye tatpar aur bolane men dhaeera aur krodha men dhaeema ho.
{1:20} kyonaki manushy ka krodha parameshvar ke dharma ka nirvah naheen kar sakata haai.
{1:21} isaliye saree malinata aur baair bhav kee baddhtee ko door karake, us vachan ko namrata se grahan kar lo, jo day men boya gaya aur jo tumhare praanon ka uddhar kar sakata haai.
{1:22} parantu vachan par chalanevale bano, aur keval sunanevale hee naheen jo apane ap ko dhaekha dete haain.
{1:23} kyonaki jo koi vachan ka sunanevala ho, aur us par chalanevala n ho, to vah us manushy ke saman haai jo apana svabhavik munh darpan men dekhta haai.
{1:24} isaliye ki vah apane ap ko dekhkar chala jata, aur turant bool jata haai ki maain kaaisa tha.
{1:25} par jo vyaktai svatantrata kee siddh vyavastha par dhyan karata rahata haai, vah apane kam men isaaliye ashaeesh paaega ki sunakar nahee, par vaaisa hee kam karata haai.
{1:26} yaadi koi apane ap ko bhkt samajhe, aur apanee jeebh par lagam n de, par apane day ko dhaekha de, to us kee bhaktai vyarth haai.
{1:27} hamare parameshvar aur pita ke nikatt shuuddh aur nirmal bhaktai yah haai, ki anathon or vidhavaon ke klesh men un kee suadhai le, aur apane ap ko sansar se nishkalank rakhen..
{2:1} he mere bhaiyo, hamare mahimayukt prabhu yeeshu maseeh ka vishvas tum men pakshapat ke sath n ho.
{2:2} kyonaki yadi aek puroosh sone ke chhlle aur sundar vasr pahine huae tumharee sabha men aae aur aek kangal bhee maaile kuchaaile kapade paahine huae aae.
{2:3} aur tum us sundar vasrvale ka munh dekhkar kaho ki too vahan achchhee jagah baaitt aur us kangal se kaho, ki too yahan khda rah, ya mere panv kee peeddhee ke pas baaitt.
{2:4} to kya tum ne apas men bhed bhav n kiya aur kuavichar se nyay karanevale n tthare?
{2:5} he mere piry bhaiyon suno kya parameshvar ne is jagat ke kangalon ko naheen chuna ki vishvas men dharmee, aur us rajy ke adhaikaree ho, jis kee pratigyaa us ne un se kee haai jo us se praem rakhte haain?
{2:6} par tum ne us kangal ka apaman kiya: kya dhanee log tum par atyachar naheen karate aur kya ve hee tumhen kachahariyon men ghaseett ghaseett kar naheen le jate?
{2:7} kya ve us uttm nam kee ninda naheen karate jis ke tum kahalaae jate ho?
{2:8} taubhee yadi tum pavitra shaasr ke is vachan ke anusar, ki too apane padosee se apane saman praem rak, sachamuch us rajy vyavastha ko pooree karate ho, to achchha karate ho.
{2:9} par yaadi tum pakshapat karate ho, to pap karate ho aur vyavastha tumhen aparadhaee ttharatee haai.
{2:10} kyonaki jo koi saree vyavastha ka palan karata haai parantu aek hee bat men chook jaae to vah sab baton me doshaee tthara.
{2:11} isaliye ki jis ne yah kaha, ki too vyaabhaichar n karana usee ne yah bhee kaha, ki too hatya n karana isaliye yaadi too ne vyaabhaichar to naheen kiya, par hatya kee taubhee too vyavastha ka ulanghan karane vala tthara.
{2:12} tum un logon kee nai vachan bolo, aur kam bhee karo, jin ka nyay svatantrata kee vyavastha ke anusar hoga.
{2:13} kyonaki jis ne daya naheen kee, usaka nyay bina daya ke hoga: daya nyay par jayavant hotee haai..
{2:14} he mere bhaiyo, yadi koi kahe ki mujhe vishvas haai par vah karma n karata ho, to us se kya labh? kya aeesa vishvas kabhee usaka uddhar kar sakata haai?
{2:15} yaadi koi bhai ya bahin nagen ughaade ho, aur unhen prati din bhojan kee ghattee ho.
{2:16} aur tum men se koi un se kahe, kushal se jao, tum garam raho aur trapt raho par jo vastuaen deh ke liye avashyak haain vah unhen n de, to kya labh?
{2:17} vaaise hee vishvas bhee, yadi karma saahit n ho to apane svabhav men mara hua haai.
{2:18} baran koi kah sakata haai ki tujhe vishvas haai, aur maain karma karata hoon: too apana vishvas mujhe karma bina to dikha aur maain apana vishvas apane karmon ke dara tujhe dikhaunga.
{2:19} tujhe vishvas haai ki aek hee parameshvar haai: too achchha karata haai: dushttatma bhee vishvas rakhte, aur thrathrate haain.
{2:20} par he nikamme manushy kya too yah bhee naheen janata, ki karma bina vishvas vyarth haai?
{2:21} jab hamare pita ibraaheem ne apane putra isahak ko vedee par chaddhaya, to kya vah karmo se dhaaarmik n tthara tha.
{2:22} so too ne dekh liya ki vishvas ne us ke kamon ke sath milakar prabhav dala haai aur karmo se vishvas siddh hua.
{2:23} aur paavitra shaasr ka yah vachan poora hua, ki ibraaheem ne parameshvar kee prateeti kee, aur yah usake liye dharma gina gaya, aur vah parameshvar ka mitra kahalaya.
{2:24} so tum ne dekh liya ki manushy keval vishvas se hee nahee, baran kamorn se bhee dharmee ttharata haai.
{2:25} vaaise hee rahab veshya bhee jab us ne dooton ko apane ghar men utara, aur doosare marga se vida kiya, to kya karmon se dhaaarmik n ttharee?
{2:26} nidan, jaaise deh atma bina maree hui haai vaaisa hee vishvas bhee karma bina mara hua haai..

{3:3} jab ham apane vash men karane ke liye ghaedon ke munh men lagam lagate haai, to ham un kee saree deh ko bhee faer sakate haain.

{3:9} isee se ham prabhu aur pita kee stuti karate haain aur isee se manushyon ko jo parameshvar ke svaroop men utpann huae haain srap dete haain.

{3:13} tum men gyaanavan aur samajhdar kaun haai? jo aeesa ho vah apane kamon ko achchhe chalachalan se us namrata sahit pragatt kare jo gyaan se utpann hotee haai.
